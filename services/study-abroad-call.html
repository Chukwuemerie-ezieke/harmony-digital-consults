FAQ

I'm not sure what course to study — can you still help?
Yes. About a third of our calls include a career-fit conversation before we shortlist universities.
Do you handle the actual application?
This call gives you a clear plan. If you'd like us to handle the full application end-to-end, we'll quote that separately after the call.
What if my budget is tight?
We highlight low-tuition countries (Hungary, Germany public unis, Malaysia, Cyprus) and scholarships you actually qualify for given Nigerian grading.
Can my parent join the call?
Yes, up to 2 people on the Zoom — students often join with a parent or guardian.
