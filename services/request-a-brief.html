Request a Custom Research Brief

Fixed-scope PDF brief in 5 business days · ₦25,000 (parent) to ₦3M (institutional)

Three brief tiers

    2–4 page PDF: "Should we enrol our child at University X / send them to TVET Y?" with risk score, alternatives, and a decision matrix. Turnaround: 3 business days.
  • Institutional brief — from ₦150,000
    6–10 page PDF for schools, foundations, or CSR teams: market sizing, programme benchmarking, or pathway design. Turnaround: 5 business days.

What we research

  • Tertiary risk — strike history, completion rates, real time-to-graduation for specific Nigerian universities.
  • TVET pathway design — choosing the right Federal Science & Technical College, sourcing equipment, integrating digital skills, tracking outcomes.
  • Programme benchmarking — how your school or foundation compares to peers on EdTech adoption, ICT, teacher skills.
  • Market sizing & investor briefs — Nigerian EdTech segments, donor landscape, regulatory environment.
