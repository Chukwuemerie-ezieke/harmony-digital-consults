# Harmony Teacher Training — Curriculum Scope

**Owner:** Harmony Digital Consults Ltd
**Last updated:** 13 May 2026
**Status:** Master scope document — built bit by bit, module by module

This document is the single source of truth for the full Harmony Teacher Training curriculum. It maps every module to the 6 competencies in the [Teacher Digital Skills Self-Assessment](https://harmonydigitalconsults.com.ng/dashboards/teacher-digital-skills-self-assessment.html) and to the [TPACK framework](https://citejournal.org/volume-9/issue-1-09/general/what-is-technological-pedagogicalcontent-knowledge/) + [UNESCO ICT-CFT v3](https://www.unesco.org/en/digital-competencies-skills/ict-cft).

## 5. How to build each module

When ready to develop a specific module, the standard outline includes:

- Learning objectives (3–5)
- Target audience & prerequisites
- Lesson breakdown (sessions, hours each)
- Activities & practical tasks
- Assessment method
- Materials & tools required
- Recommended affiliate tools (where applicable — track in Affiliate Link Tracker sheet)
- Trainer notes
