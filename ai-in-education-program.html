FAQ

Quick Answers

When does the next cohort start?

Cohorts run quarterly. After you register, we email the exact start date and class schedule for the next available cohort.

How much time do I need each week?

Plan for about 2.5 hours per week — one live class plus a short practical task. Recordings let you catch up if you miss a session.

Is the certificate recognised?

The certificate is issued by Harmony Digital Consults Ltd, an EdTech consulting firm. We are pursuing partnerships with teacher associations and TRCN-aligned bodies for added recognition.

Can I get a refund?

Full refund up to 48 hours before the cohort starts. After Week 1 begins, refunds are not available, but you may defer once to the next cohort.

What if my internet is unreliable?

All sessions are recorded and replays remain available for 8 weeks. WhatsApp updates and downloadable templates keep you on track without live attendance.

Can my school enrol several teachers?

Yes. Choose the School Cohort option and we will send a tailored quote starting at ₦25,000 per teacher for 10+ enrolments.
