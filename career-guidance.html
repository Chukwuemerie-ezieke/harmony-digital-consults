Career Guidance Platform

Personalised university course recommendations, JAMB subject combinations, and career paths for Nigerian secondary school students.

What It Does

The Career Guidance Platform is a free, interactive web tool that helps Nigerian SS3 students discover their ideal university course based on their strongest subjects, interests, and preferences. It analyses each student's unique combination and provides personalised recommendations — including JAMB subject requirements, O'Level prerequisites, JAMB cut-off marks, top universities, and related career paths.

For Students

SS3 students choosing their university course can discover 3 personalised recommendations based on their strengths and interests — with JAMB requirements and career prospects for each.

For Counsellors

School counsellors can review all student submissions from the Counsellor Dashboard — search, filter by recommended course, and view each student's detailed report.

For Schools

Schools can embed the platform on their website or share the direct link with students. No installation required — it works on any device with a browser.

Usage Guide

How It Works

Students complete a simple 3-step form. The platform analyses their profile and generates personalised recommendations instantly.

1

About You

Enter your first name, select your class, and choose your top 3 strongest subjects from the available WAEC/NECO subjects. This tells the platform what you excel at.

2

Your Interests

Select the areas that genuinely interest you — technology, healthcare, art, business, law, and more. Pick as many as apply. The platform uses these alongside your subjects for better matching.

3

Preferences

Choose your preferred university type (Federal, State, or Private), your preferred state, and your current grade range. These fine-tune the recommendations to your situation.

4

Your Results

Receive 3 personalised course recommendations — each with JAMB subject combination, O'Level requirements, cut-off marks, top universities, career options, and a personal message explaining why it suits you. Download your results as a PDF.

Detailed Results

What Each Recommendation Includes

Every recommendation is comprehensive and actionable — not just a course name, but everything a student needs to plan their next step.

Course Description

What the course covers, what you'll study, and what the degree prepares you for.

JAMB Subjects

The exact 4 UTME subject combination required for admission to this course.

O'Level Requirements

The specific WAEC/NECO credit passes needed — so you know if you qualify.

Cut-off Marks

JAMB cut-off marks for Federal, State, and Private universities offering this course.

Top Universities

5 recommended universities offering the course — including university type (Federal, State, Private).

Career Paths

Real career options graduates pursue — from professional roles to industry opportunities.
