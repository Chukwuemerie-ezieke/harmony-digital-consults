Harmony TimeGrid

Smart school timetable management with auto-schedule generation, conflict detection, and Nigerian curriculum support for seamless academic planning.

What It Does

Harmony TimeGrid eliminates the manual headaches of school timetable creation. It's an interactive timetable generator that auto-assigns subjects to periods across all class arms while detecting and resolving teacher clashes. Built specifically for Nigerian secondary schools, it supports the standard 8-period day structure with assembly, devotion, short breaks, and lunch periods built in. Schools can generate complete weekly timetables for all classes in seconds, then fine-tune with drag-and-drop editing.

For Vice Principals (Academics)

Generate conflict-free timetables for all class arms in one click. Review the master timetable view and resolve any clashes before the term begins.

For Teachers

View your personal teaching schedule across all assigned classes. Know exactly when and where you're teaching each day of the week.

For School Administrators

Export timetables as PDF (with school branding), CSV, or Excel for official records, noticeboards, and parent communication.

Features

Key Features

Timetable generation built for the structure of Nigerian secondary schools.

Auto-Assignment Engine

One-click timetable generation for all classes. Even distribution of subjects across the week with a maximum of 2 appearances per day per subject.

Clash Detection

Automatic teacher clash detection across all classes. Clashes highlighted in red with suggested fixes to resolve conflicts instantly.

Drag-and-Drop Editing

Fine-tune generated timetables by dragging and dropping periods. Swap subjects between slots with visual feedback and real-time clash checking.

Master Timetable View

View all classes side-by-side per day. Cross-class teacher indicators ensure no teacher is double-booked anywhere in the school.

Nigerian School Structure

Pre-configured for 8 periods/day with Assembly/Devotion, Short Break, and Lunch Break. Supports JSS1-SS3 with multiple arms (A, B, C, etc.).

Export & Print

Export as branded PDF with school header and logo, CSV for admin records, or Excel spreadsheet. Ready for noticeboards and official filing.
