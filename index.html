About Us

Building the Future of Nigerian Education

Harmony Digital Consults Ltd is a specialized IT consulting firm dedicated to transforming education through innovative technology solutions across Nigeria. Incorporated on October 25, 2025, we deliver comprehensive educational technology services to schools, institutions, and corporate organizations.

Our portfolio spans e-learning platform implementation, custom software development, school management systems, digital transformation consulting, cloud computing solutions, cybersecurity, and corporate IT training programs — all designed for the Nigerian context.
